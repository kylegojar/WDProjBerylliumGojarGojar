... Wait, what IS P.I.E?

Description: P.I.E is defined as the common ancestor of the Indo-European Languages. It's geographically spread out, extending between most of Europe and nearly half of Asia. Their influence is so dominant, Basque, Turkish, and the Uralic languages (shown above) are the few exclusions in Europe. For more info, navigate through the sidebar!
